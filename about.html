About Me

Who I Am

I'm an economist and data scientist who specializes in combining rigorous economic theory with modern computational methods. My work spans microeconomic and macroeconomic analysis, econometric modeling, industrial organization, and international trade—all approached through the lens of both theoretical frameworks and empirical investigation using advanced statistical techniques.

My diverse background—from U.S. Marine Corps service to student judicial leadership to quantitative research—has cultivated not only technical expertise but also essential soft skills: leadership, effective communication, critical thinking, and the ability to work under pressure. Whether managing maintenance operations for F-18 aircraft, adjudicating constitutional cases, or conducting econometric analysis, I bring discipline, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence.

I'm driven by the intersection of economic theory and technology. By leveraging tools like Python, Stata, and R alongside deep theoretical understanding, I transform complex economic questions into tractable empirical analyses. My goal is to bridge the gap between abstract economic models and real-world data, producing insights that are both theoretically sound and practically applicable.

My Approach

I believe in the power of combining theoretical rigor with empirical precision. Economic theory provides the framework for understanding complex phenomena—whether market dynamics, trade relationships, or labor market outcomes. Modern computational tools and econometric methods allow me to test these theories against real-world data, generating insights that are both theoretically grounded and empirically validated.

My diverse background shapes this approach. Military service taught me discipline, attention to detail, and the importance of rigorous procedures. Student government experience developed my ability to communicate complex ideas, mediate disagreements, and make principled decisions. Together, these experiences enable me to tackle challenging research questions, collaborate across disciplines, and communicate findings effectively to technical and non-technical audiences alike.

Whether investigating international trade dynamics, forecasting economic trends, or analyzing market structures, I bring curiosity, methodical analysis, and a commitment to producing work that meets the highest standards of economic research.
