Economic Volatility and Market Microstructure in Emerging Economies

2024 Team of 3
Published

This peer-reviewed study examines the relationship between economic volatility and high-frequency trading patterns in emerging markets. Using econometric models and machine learning classification, we identify previously undocumented transmission mechanisms between macroeconomic shocks and microstructure dynamics.

Methodology

  • Vector Autoregression (VAR) analysis
  • Granger causality testing
  • Machine learning classification
  • Time-series forecasting

Key Findings

  • Novel volatility transmission mechanisms
  • Market efficiency implications
  • Policy recommendations for regulators
  • Quantified risk metrics for traders

Labor Market Dynamics and Skills Mismatch: A Regional Analysis

2023 Lead Researcher
In Progress

An ongoing longitudinal study investigating regional labor market dynamics and skills mismatches across the United States. Using occupational data, wage information, and educational attainment records, we construct novel measures of skills alignment and test competing theoretical frameworks.

Data Sources

  • American Community Survey (2009-2023)
  • Occupational Employment Statistics
  • Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • Census microdata files

Analysis Approach

  • Cluster analysis for skill categorization
  • Panel regression models
  • Spatial econometric methods
  • Longitudinal tracking

Causal Inference in Policy Evaluation: A Comparative Study

2023 Collaborative study, 4 authors
Published

Systematic comparison of causal inference methodologies (DID, RDD, IV, and matching estimators) applied to public policy evaluation. Using Monte Carlo simulations and real-world policy data, we assess robustness, efficiency, and practical implementation considerations.

Methodologies Evaluated

  • Difference-in-Differences (DID)
  • Regression Discontinuity (RDD)
  • Instrumental Variables (IV)
  • Matching & Propensity Score

Applications

  • Education policy interventions
  • Healthcare program evaluation
  • Employment training effects
  • Tax policy impacts
